## The SparkGood Opportunity

### Market Validation
- **$2 trillion** annual social enterprise revenue globally
- **10 million** social enterprises worldwide
- **200+ million** jobs created by social ventures
- **$1.57 trillion** in impact investing assets (21% CAGR)
- **Record startup formation:** 5.5M new businesses in 2023

### Target Audience Confirmation
- **66%** of 18-35 year-olds have started or plan side hustles
- **60%** of entrepreneurs prioritize social/environmental impact over profitability
- Gen Z/Millennials are **most likely** to start purpose-driven ventures
- Diverse founders (women, minorities) show **higher entrepreneurial optimism**

### Problem Validation: The Intention-Action Gap
- **70%** of Americans care deeply about social causes
- **80%** globally want governments to take stronger climate action
- **BUT few translate care into action**
- When people learn the scale of problems + see clear pathways: donations increase **55-61%**

**SparkGood fills this gap:** Bridge intention → action with guided, accessible tools

## Design Direction Validated

### "Campfire Energy" Principles:
- Warm, grounded, mentorship-feeling
- Conversational flows, not forms
- Amber/gold (#F59E0B) as primary accent
- Deep charcoal (#1C1412) backgrounds
- Typography: Playfair Display (headings), DM Sans (body)
- Mobile-first, progress indicators, micro-rewards

### Key Design Patterns to Implement:
1. **Clear mission statement** front and center
2. **Prominent CTA** (amber button, high contrast)
3. **One question at a time** (conversational)
4. **Progress bar** visible throughout flow
5. **Celebration moments** at completions
6. **Real imagery** or custom illustrations
7. **Trust signals** and transparency

### Inspirational Sites:
- charitywater.org — Conversion-focused, emotional
- prometai.app — Fast plan generation UX
- headspace.com — Gentle onboarding
